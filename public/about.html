About Our Fellowship

"For where two or three gather in my name, there am I with them." - Matthew 18:20

Our Story

Deeper Life Campus Fellowship has been a beacon of hope and spiritual growth in the Ado Ekiti community for years. Located behind Trinity Hospital along Adebayo Road, our fellowship began as a small group of committed believers who sought to create a space where students and young adults could grow in their faith.

What started as intimate gatherings has blossomed into a vibrant community that welcomes all who seek to know Christ more deeply. Our fellowship is built on the foundation of God's Word, characterized by authentic worship, meaningful relationships, and a commitment to serving both our local community and the broader world.

We believe that every person who walks through our doors is precious to God, and we strive to create an environment where everyone can experience His love, find their purpose, and grow in their relationship with Jesus Christ.

What We Believe

The Holy Scripture

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God, our ultimate authority for faith and practice, and the foundation for all spiritual growth and understanding.

Salvation Through Christ

We believe that salvation comes through faith in Jesus Christ alone, by grace through faith, not by works, and that He died for our sins and rose again for our justification.

The Trinity

We believe in one God who exists eternally in three persons: the Father, the Son (Jesus Christ), and the Holy Spirit, each fully God yet one divine essence.

Christian Living

We believe in living a life that honors God through holiness, love, service to others, and faithful stewardship of the gifts and resources He has entrusted to us.

The Great Commission

We believe in actively sharing the Gospel and making disciples of all nations, as commanded by our Lord Jesus Christ before His ascension.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To create a loving community where students and young adults can encounter God's transforming love, grow in their faith, and be equipped to serve Christ in their daily lives.

We are committed to providing biblical teaching, meaningful fellowship, and practical support as our members navigate the challenges and opportunities of campus life and beyond.

Our Vision

To see a generation of young people who are deeply rooted in God's Word, passionately committed to His kingdom, and actively engaged in transforming their communities through Christ's love.

We envision our fellowship as a launching pad for lifelong disciples who will carry the light of Christ wherever God calls them to serve.

Why Join Our Fellowship?

Authentic Community

Experience genuine relationships built on Christ's love. Our fellowship provides a safe space where you can be yourself, share your struggles, and celebrate God's goodness together with fellow believers.

Biblical Teaching

Grow in your understanding of God's Word through expository preaching, interactive Bible studies, and practical application of Scripture to everyday life challenges.

Spiritual Growth

Discover your spiritual gifts, develop your relationship with God, and learn to walk in the power of the Holy Spirit through our discipleship programs and mentorship opportunities.

Service Opportunities

Put your faith into action through various ministry opportunities, community outreach programs, and evangelism efforts that make a real difference in people's lives.
